Olive oil 'Vanja' once again awarded with the Grand Mention diploma on Leone d'Oro by Mastri Oleary competition in Italy

By the end of June the Italian association Mastri Oleary that organises perhaps the most prestige extra virgin olive oil competition on the world informed us that our extra virgin olive oil was awarded with the Grand Mention Diploma (Diploma di Gran Menzione), this time in the category of intensive fruitiness. Fourteen oils from all over the world received an award in this category.

July 18, 2008
