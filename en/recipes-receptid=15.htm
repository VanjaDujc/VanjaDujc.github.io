Pancakes with dried kaki and cottage cheese

Ingredients:

For pancakes:

  • 250 gr flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2,5dl milk
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons of sugar
For cottage cheese cream:
  • 250 gr cottage cheese
  • 1 dl sweet cream
  • 1 vanilla stalk
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • grinded peel of a lemon
  • dried kaki (Japanese persimmons) in orangle flavoured olive oil

Instructions:
Divide yolks from whites. Beat the whites until they become firm. Add sugar to the yolks and beat well. While beating, add flour, salt, and milk until the batter gets smooth. Add whites, stir well and leave for about 15 minutes. Fry the pancakes and spread them with the cottage cheese cream.

Put the sweet cream in a small pot. Cut vanilla stalk in half longitudinally, carve out the seeds, add them to the cream, cut the remaining vanilla stalk in 3 or 4 pieces and ad them to the cream. Heat the cream slightly. Leave for about 15 minutes, than remove the pieces of vanilla stalk from the cream. Put the cottage cheese in a dish, add honey, sweet cream, grinded peel of a lemon and stir well. Chop kaki on small pieces. Spread the cream on each pancake, sparkle with chopped kaki and drizzle with orange flavoured olive oil. Serve hot.
