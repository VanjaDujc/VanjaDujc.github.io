"Cacciucco Vanja" with potatoes on the Istrian way

Ingredients:

For cacciucco:

  • 1,5 kg of fresh cuttlefish (squid)
  • 8 shrimps
  • ¼ cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 4 dried tomatoes in extra virgin olive oil "Vanja"
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper in grains
  • 1 cup white wine
  • at least 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon breadcrumbs
For potatoes on the Istrian way:
  • 8 potatoes, peeled
  • ¼ cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled

Instructions:
Clean the cuttlefish under the water and cut it on larger pieces. Be sure that you preserve the ink. Put the extra virgin olive oil in a pan, add garlic, pepper, parsley, cuttlefish, shrimps and the ink preserved to the cold oil and heat over 5-10 minutes on a low temperature. Add dried tomato, oregano, rosemary, wine, water, a pinch of salt and bay leaf, raise the heat to a medium temperature and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring from time to time. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs in order to get the dish more solid and stir well. Serve hot.

Cut potatoes to 4 pieces and salt them. Cut each garlic clove horizontally on 4 pieces. Put the extra virgin olive oil in a pan, add garlic and potatoes to cold oil and let them fry on a medium temperature. At the beginning stir well in order that you prevent potatoes and garlic from sticking to the pan. When potatoes begin to stick, stop stirring and leave the dish simmer with the cover covered. From time to time (every 5 minutes), turn them gently with fish slice or spatula. Continue simmering potatoes for at least 20 minutes or until they are entirely soft.
