Olive oils 'Vanja' achieved remarkable success on international competitions

In spring the important assesments of extra virgin olive oils are going on. We have the pleasure to announce that our olive oils had great success on important competitions in Europe and America. On international competition ‘Olive Oil Award Zurich 2008’ in Switzerland ( http://oliveoilaward.ch ) our single varietal olive oil 'Maurino' received the Silver Olive. In Montreal, Canada, on the competition ‘Olive d'Or’, http://www.sialmontreal.com/olivedorawards.ch2,  ‘Maurino’ won the award Silver drop. In 2007 on the same competition it received the Bronze drop. We are especially proud of the Silver drop because only three awards are given in each category (light, medium and and strong flavour)on Olive d’Or.

Our olive oils came back with awards also from our neighbour country Croatia. On ‘Nočnjak’, an international competition that was organised in Primošten this year, our extra virgin olive oil received the title of Vice Champion. On the competition ‘Maslina’ in Split our lemon flavoured olive oil was named the absolute winner in the cathegory of flavoured olive oils for the third year.

May 8, 2008
