Vanilla ice cream with dried kaki (Japanese Persimmons) in orange flavoured olive oil

Ingredients:

  • 400 g vanilla ice cream
  • 4 tablespoons of dried kaki in orange olive oil

Instructions:
Chop khaki on small pieces. Arrange the ice cream on the plates, sprinkle with dried khaki and drizzle with orange flavoured olive oil.
